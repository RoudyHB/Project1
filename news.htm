Amr Diab's Spectacular Return to Beirut

On June 15th, 2024, the legendary Egyptian singer Amr Diab captivated a massive audience at Beirut Waterfront Arena. This marked his second consecutive annual performance in Lebanon after a decade-long hiatus, leaving fans exhilarated with his timeless hits and dynamic stage presence.

Lebanon Latin Festival 2024

Dance enthusiasts gathered for the Lebanon Latin Festival in September 2024, enjoying workshops, performances, and parties celebrating Latin music and dance styles. The festival attracted participants from around the world, fostering cultural exchange and vibrant performances.

Byblos International Festival 2024

The historic city of Byblos hosted its renowned International Festival in July 2024, featuring performances by acclaimed artists across various genres. The festival's unique setting by the ancient harbor provided an unforgettable experience for attendees.
