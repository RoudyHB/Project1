About Us

Who We Are

We are passionate individuals dedicated to creating unforgettable musical experiences. This event is proudly organized by the Rotary Club of Beirut Cedars, bringing together talented musicians and an enthusiastic audience for a night of symphonic rock like never before.

Our Mission

Our mission is to merge the power of rock with the beauty of symphonic music, offering a unique concert experience that not only entertains but also supports a greater cause. We strive to bring people together through music while making a positive impact on society.

Our Vision

We envision a world where music transcends barriers and brings communities closer. Through our symphonic rock concerts, we aim to inspire, unite, and create lasting memories for all who attend, while also supporting charitable initiatives that make a difference.

Our Goals

Our goal is to organize high-quality musical events that leave a lasting impression on our audience. We are committed to supporting AFEL, a foundation dedicated to helping vulnerable children, by donating a portion of the concert proceeds to their cause. Through this, we hope to spread joy, awareness, and positive change.
